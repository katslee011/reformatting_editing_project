How to Draw a Jack-o'-Lantern

Materials needed

  • Paper
  • Pencil
  • Eraser

Caution

Use the pencil lightly; drawing too thickly might make the pencil lines harder to erase later.

Key

If the writing is BLACK, draw with a PENCIL

If the writing is BLUE, draw with a PEN

If the writing is RED, erase with an ERASER

Instructions

How to draw the pumpkin

  1. Drawing the guidelines
    1. Draw a circle
    2. Draw four vertical lines to divide the circle into five sections
  2. Drawing the pumpkin
    1. Connect the points where the line and circle intersect with slight arcs from the top to the bottom of the circle
    2. Connect the points where the line and circle intersect going around the circle
    3. Trace the new shape with pen
    4. Lightly trace the arcs in the circle with pen
    5. Erase the straight pencil lines

How to draw the pumpkin face

  1. Drawing the guidelines
    1. Draw two horizontal lines to divide the pumpkin into thirds
    2. Draw a horizontal line to divide the pumpkin in half
    3. Draw two vertical lines to divide the pumpkin into thirds again
    4. Draw a vertical line to divide the pumpkin in half
  2. Drawing the face
    1. Draw a triangle on the rightmost, topmost point of intersection
    2. Draw another triangle on the leftmost, topmost point
    3. Draw a semicircle on the bottom of each triangle
    4. Draw a triangle on the centermost point
    5. Draw a semicircle between the left and right vertical lines
    6. Draw a zigzag pattern with five teeth
    7. Draw another zigzag pattern with four bottom teeth
    8. Trace the facial features
    9. Erase the pencil
    10. Color in the facial features

How to draw the stem

  1. Draw a small rectangle at the top of the pumpkin
  2. Draw a loop-de-loop next to the rectangle

Congrats, you’ve successfully drawn a jack-o’-lantern!
