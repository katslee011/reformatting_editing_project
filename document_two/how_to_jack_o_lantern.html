How to Draw a Jack-o'-Lantern

Materials needed

  • Paper
  • Pencil
  • Eraser

Caution

Use the pencil lightly; drawing too thickly might make the pencil lines harder to erase later.

Key

If the writing is BLACK, draw with a PENCIL

If the writing is BLUE, draw with a PEN

If the writing is RED, erase with an ERASER
